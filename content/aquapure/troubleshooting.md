# AquaPure Troubleshooting Guide

The three most common issues with AquaPure systems are low water pressure, a strange taste, and leaking. Most are solved in a few minutes without a service visit.

## Low water pressure from the filter tap

1. **Check the inlet valve.** After a cartridge change it is often left half-open — turn it fully counter-clockwise.
2. **Check the cartridge age.** A clogged cartridge is the most common cause. If it has been more than 6 months (or the Home's indicator light is orange), replace the cartridge.
3. **Check for kinked tubing.** Pull the unit forward and straighten any bent tube runs behind the cabinet.
4. **AquaPure Pro only:** if the storage tank empties quickly, the tank pre-charge may be low. With the tank empty, measure the air valve with a tire gauge — it should read 0.5 bar. Top it up with a hand pump if needed.

If pressure is still low after these steps, contact support@aquapure.example with your model name and purchase date.

## Water tastes or smells strange

- **New system or new cartridge:** a slightly gray tint or bitter taste in the first days is harmless carbon dust. Flush the system for 10 minutes and it will clear.
- **Chlorine taste returning:** the carbon cartridge is exhausted — replace it.
- **Metallic taste:** usually comes from the building's own pipes upstream of the filter. Run the unfiltered tap for one minute in the morning before using the filter tap.
- **Rotten-egg smell (Pro):** the storage tank needs sanitizing. Order the free sanitizing kit from our webshop and follow the included 20-minute procedure.

## The system is leaking

First, put a towel under the unit and shut the inlet valve. Then find the source:

1. **Drips at a quick-connect fitting:** push the tube firmly into the collet until it seats, then pull gently to lock. If it still drips, cut 1 cm off the tube end squarely and reinsert.
2. **Drips from the filter head:** the cartridge is not fully rotated into place — twist it a quarter turn until it clicks.
3. **Drips from the housing itself:** the O-ring may be pinched. Remove the cartridge, reseat the O-ring with a drop of food-grade silicone grease (included in the box), and reinstall.

A cracked housing or persistent leak is covered by the 2-year warranty — email a photo to support@aquapure.example and we will ship a replacement part within two business days.
