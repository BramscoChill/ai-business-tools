# AquaPure Product Guide

AquaPure Water Systems sells three under-sink water filtration models. All models connect to a standard cold-water line and come with a first filter cartridge in the box.

## AquaPure Compact — €149

The Compact is our entry model for apartments and small households (1–2 people). It uses a single-stage activated carbon filter that removes chlorine, sediment, and common taste and odor issues. Flow rate is 2 liters per minute, and the unit fits in cabinets as shallow as 25 cm. The Compact uses the C10 filter cartridge (€29), which should be replaced every 6 months. Installation is tool-free with the included quick-connect fittings.

## AquaPure Home — €299

The Home is our most popular model, sized for families of 3–5 people. It is a two-stage system: stage one is a sediment pre-filter that protects the main filter from sand and rust particles, and stage two is a high-capacity carbon block that removes chlorine, lead, pesticides, and microplastics. Flow rate is 3.5 liters per minute. The Home uses the H20 twin cartridge set (€49), replaced every 6 months. It includes a filter-life indicator light on the faucet that turns from blue to orange when a replacement is due.

## AquaPure Pro — €549

The Pro is a three-stage reverse osmosis system for households that want the highest level of purification, including removal of nitrates, fluoride, hardness minerals, and dissolved solids. It produces up to 190 liters of purified water per day and includes a 5-liter pressurized storage tank so filtered water is instantly available. The Pro uses the RO50 membrane (€89, replaced every 24 months) plus the P5 pre- and post-filter set (€59, replaced every 6 months). The Pro requires a drain connection for the reject water line.

## Which model should I choose?

- Choose the **Compact** if you mainly want better-tasting water on a budget.
- Choose the **Home** if you want protection against lead and microplastics for a family.
- Choose the **Pro** if you have hard water, use well water, or want maximum purity for drinking and cooking.

All prices include VAT. Every model ships with free delivery in the Netherlands and Belgium, a printed installation guide, and access to our support team at support@aquapure.example.
