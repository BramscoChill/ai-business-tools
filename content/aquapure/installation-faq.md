# AquaPure Installation & Maintenance FAQ

## How do I install my AquaPure system?

All AquaPure models are designed for self-installation in 30–45 minutes with no special tools:

1. Turn off the cold-water valve under the sink and open the tap to release pressure.
2. Mount the filter bracket on the cabinet wall using the supplied screws, leaving at least 8 cm below the unit so cartridges can be swapped later.
3. Connect the cold-water line to the filter inlet using the quick-connect T-adapter (hand-tighten only — do not use a wrench on plastic fittings).
4. Connect the outlet tube to the included dedicated faucet, or to your existing tap with the diverter kit.
5. For the AquaPure Pro only: attach the reject-water tube to the drain saddle above the siphon.
6. Open the water valve and flush the system for 10 minutes before first use to rinse out harmless carbon dust.

If you prefer professional installation, we can arrange it during checkout for a fixed fee of €79 anywhere in the Netherlands and Belgium.

## How often do I replace the filter cartridge?

The replacement schedule depends on the model:

- **AquaPure Compact:** replace the C10 cartridge every 6 months.
- **AquaPure Home:** replace the H20 twin cartridge set every 6 months. The faucet indicator light turns orange when a replacement is due.
- **AquaPure Pro:** replace the P5 pre- and post-filter set every 6 months, and the RO50 membrane every 24 months.

Replacing a cartridge takes about five minutes: shut the inlet valve, twist the old cartridge a quarter turn to release it, click the new one in, and flush for two minutes. No tools are needed. You can order replacement cartridges in our webshop with a subscription discount of 15%.

## What does the warranty cover?

Every AquaPure system includes a 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in the housing, valves, fittings, and faucet. The warranty does not cover filter cartridges (consumables), damage from freezing, or damage caused by water pressure above 6 bar — install the included pressure reducer if your home pressure is higher. Registering your system on our website within 30 days extends the warranty to 3 years at no cost. Warranty claims are handled by our support team at support@aquapure.example and are typically resolved within two business days with a repair kit or replacement unit.
